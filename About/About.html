Nepwears — A Movement Born from Unity, Style, and Vision

Nepwears isn’t just a fashion label — it's the story of seven creative minds coming together with a shared dream. Led by Bhupendra Sapkota, the team — Aarya Ghimire, Anish Niraula, Nikhil Udas, Pujan Basnet, Shailja Limbu, and Netra Maya Thapa Magar — represents a dynamic blend of talent, vision, and pure passion.

Rooted in Nepal, inspired by the world — Nepwears was born from late-night discussions, shared ambitions, and the belief that fashion can be more than clothing — it can be a statement. From sketching ideas in cafes to diving into fabric markets, the team collaborates at every step, driven by a common goal: To bring Nepal to the forefront of global fashion.

Each piece we create carries the energy of our diverse team — different perspectives, one united spirit. Together, we’re not just building a brand — we’re building a legacy.
